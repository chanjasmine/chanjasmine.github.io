The Master and Margarita

For this Unessay, I am focusing on the question - "Can Woland be seen as somehow doing God's will, either knowingly or unknowingly?"

Main Points

There are two main arguments that can be made for this:

  • Woland is often seen as an individual playing a role in a larger plan
  • Despite the fact that he is Satan, Woland often only delivers judgement for sinners and those against God's will

These ideas will be explored further in other sections.
